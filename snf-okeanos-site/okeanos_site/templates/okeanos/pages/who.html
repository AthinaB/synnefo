~okeanos will be soon delivering production-quality IaaS to the Greek Academic and Research Community. Students, professors and researchers can dive into the 'ocean' and get the full power of virtual infrastructures (computing, network, storage). We run the service from our datacenters, VMs and storage (soon) over our dark fiber network.

Are you a student/researcher/faculty member? You can access ~okeanos using the same credentials you use to login to your University/Research Center.

Who is behind ~okeanos?

~okeanos is being designed and developed by the Greek Research and Technology Network (GRNET) for the benefit of the Greek academic community. You are welcomed to join ~okeanos and stay!
